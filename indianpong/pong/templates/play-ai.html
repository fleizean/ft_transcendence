About the Game

How to Play the Game?

In Pong game, players engage in a table tennis match against their opponents. The W-A-S-D keys (or up-down-left-right arrow keys) are used to control the ball.

Win and Improve

You earn Pong Points with every game you win. With these points, you can purchase new items, rackets, and tables from the store to enhance your gaming experience.

Get Started Now

You can create an account to personalize your gaming experience. With an account, you can track your statistics and see your position in the rankings.

More Features

You can participate in tournaments to face off against your opponents and test your skills. Additionally, you can create private games with your friends and join the Pong community.
